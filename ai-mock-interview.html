Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI mock interview practice?
AI mock interview practice uses artificial intelligence to simulate real job interviews. Simpatico HR's Career Lab conducts voice-based interviews with adaptive follow-up questions, evaluates your responses, and provides detailed scoring and feedback to help you improve.
How does the AI interviewer work?
You select your target job role and start a hands-free voice interview. The AI asks role-specific questions, listens to your answers using speech recognition, asks intelligent follow-ups, and generates a comprehensive performance report with scores and improvement tips.
Which job roles can I practice for?
Career Lab supports 15+ job roles including Software Engineering, Marketing, Finance, Healthcare, Sales, Project Management, Data Science, Design, HR, and more. The AI adapts its questions based on your selected role and difficulty level.
Can I practice in my native language?
Yes! Career Lab supports 12+ languages including English, Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Arabic, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, and Chinese. Practice in whichever language you're most comfortable with.
What does the performance report include?
Your report includes an overall score, per-question breakdown, communication quality assessment, content relevance scores, identified strengths, areas for improvement, and specific tips to boost your performance in real interviews.
