About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is a professionally established job consultancy and HR consultancy in Kerala, headquartered in Perinthalmanna. Founded in 2013, we have been supporting organizations and job seekers with structured recruitment and HR consulting solutions tailored to evolving workforce needs.

As a trusted HR consultancy in Kerala, we work closely with employers across all districts of Kerala to improve hiring outcomes through well-defined recruitment strategies, recruitment process outsourcing (RPO), and HR advisory services. Our approach focuses on quality hiring, compliance, and long-term organizational success.

Our expertise spans IT and non-IT recruitment, employer branding, recruitment system implementation, job portals, and end-to-end recruitment consulting. We support companies of all sizes with professional hiring advisory aligned with business objectives.

In addition to serving as a leading job consultancy in Kerala, Simpatico HR Consultancy provides international HR consultancy and recruitment advisory services for organizations operating in global markets. Our international consulting support extends to clients in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, Italy, France, Netherlands, Austria, United Arab Emirates (UAE), Saudi Arabia, and Qatar .

With a strong understanding of regional and international hiring standards, we assist global clients with recruitment strategy, talent evaluation, and recruitment process optimization while maintaining transparency, confidentiality, and ethical recruitment practices.

Simpatico HR Consultancy is committed to delivering dependable recruitment and HR consulting services that help organizations build capable teams and help professionals advance their careers with confidence.
