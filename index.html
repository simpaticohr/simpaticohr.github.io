About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is a professional job consultancy and HR consultancy based in Perinthalmanna, Kerala. Since 2013, we have been serving job seekers and employers across all districts of Kerala including Malappuram, Kozhikode, Ernakulam, Thrissur, Palakkad, Kannur, Wayanad, Kollam and Trivandrum.

In addition to Kerala and India, we support international HR and recruitment requirements for companies in the USA, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, Italy, France, Netherlands, Austria, UAE, Saudi Arabia and Qatar.
