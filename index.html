About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Established in 2013 and based in Perinthalmanna, Kerala, Simpatico HR Consultancy provides expert job consultancy and HR services for individuals and businesses. We support career seekers and employers across Kerala and offer international HR advisory services including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, Italy, France, Netherlands, Austria, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar and more.

With years of recruitment experience, we bridge candidates to opportunities and help employers build efficient HR systems. Our priority is ethical, transparent, and effective HR support from hiring strategy to onboarding.
