About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is a professional job consultancy and HR consultancy based in Perinthalmanna, Kerala, serving candidates and employers across all districts of Kerala including Malappuram, Kozhikode, Ernakulam, Thrissur, Palakkad, Kannur, Wayanad, Kollam and Thiruvananthapuram.

Established in 2013, we deliver structured recruitment and HR solutions while helping job seekers build meaningful careers.

Beyond Kerala and India, we support recruitment and HR requirements for organizations in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, Italy, France, Netherlands, Austria, UAE, Saudi Arabia and Qatar.
