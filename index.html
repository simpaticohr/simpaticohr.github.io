About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is a professional job consultancy and HR consultancy based in Perinthalmanna, Kerala, serving job seekers and employers across all districts of Kerala including Malappuram, Kozhikode, Ernakulam, Thrissur, Palakkad, Kannur, Wayanad, Kollam and Trivandrum.

Established in 2013, we provide recruitment and HR consulting services focused on quality hiring, long-term workforce planning and employer branding. Our approach combines practical HR expertise with structured recruitment processes.

In addition to Kerala and India, Simpatico HR Consultancy supports international HR and recruitment requirements for companies in the USA, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, Italy, France, Netherlands, Austria, UAE, Saudi Arabia and Qatar.
