About

Simpatico HR Consultancy is a professional job consultancy and HR consultancy based in Perinthalmanna, Kerala, serving candidates and employers across all districts of Kerala. Established in 2013, we support organizations with structured recruitment and HR solutions while helping job seekers find meaningful career opportunities.

Beyond Kerala and India, we also work with clients and recruitment requirements from USA, UK, Canada, Europe, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Ireland, France, Netherlands, Switzerland and other global markets. Our approach focuses on quality hiring, compliance, and long-term workforce alignment.

Services

Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

End-to-end recruitment solutions for companies in Kerala, India and international locations. We assist with sourcing, screening and hiring suitable candidates across industries.

Job Consultancy Services

Career support and job placement assistance for candidates seeking opportunities in Kerala, across India and overseas markets including GCC, Europe and North America.

HR Consultancy

HR advisory services covering hiring processes, workforce planning, documentation support and HR best practices for growing organizations.

Career Guidance & Interview Support

Personalized career guidance, resume review and interview preparation to help candidates improve employability and confidence.
