About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is a professionally established job consultancy and HR consultancy in Kerala, headquartered in Perinthalmanna. Founded in 2013, we support organizations and job seekers with structured recruitment and HR consulting solutions.

We work with employers across all districts of Kerala, helping improve hiring outcomes through recruitment strategy, recruitment process outsourcing (RPO), and HR advisory services.

In addition to Kerala-based services, we provide international HR consultancy and recruitment advisory for clients in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, Italy, France, Netherlands, Austria, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Qatar.

Our commitment is to ethical recruitment, transparency, and long-term workforce success.
