Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment methods do you support?
India: UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking, wallets via Cashfree.
International: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and more via Paddle. Tax compliance handled automatically.
How does the 2-day free trial work?
When you register your company, you get 2 days of full access to all Professional features. No credit card required. After the trial, upgrade to continue.
Can I change plans later?
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Yes! Annual billing saves you 2 months (≈17% off). Toggle the billing switch above to see annual pricing.
Is my payment data secure?
Absolutely. We never store your card details. Cashfree (PCI DSS Level 1) handles Indian payments and Paddle (Merchant of Record) handles international payments with full tax compliance.
