Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 2-day free trial work?
When you register your company, you get 2 days of full access to all Professional features. No credit card required. After the trial, you'll need to upgrade to continue using the platform.
Can I change plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately. Contact our sales team for plan modifications.
What happens to my data after the trial?
Your data is safe. After the trial expires, you can still view your data in read-only mode. Once you upgrade, full access is restored instantly.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Yes! Annual billing gives you 2 months free. Contact our sales team for annual pricing and custom plans.
