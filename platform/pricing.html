Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment methods do you support?
India: UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking, and wallets via CCAvenue (PCI DSS Level 1). International: Bank transfer via Wise for USD payments.
How does the 2-day free trial work?
When you register your company, you get 2 days of full access to all Professional features. No credit card required. After the trial, upgrade to continue.
How do international payments work?
International customers pay in USD via Wise bank transfer. After subscribing, you'll receive our Wise account details and a unique reference code. Your subscription activates once the transfer is confirmed (typically within 1-2 business days).
Is my payment data secure?
Absolutely. We never store your card details. CCAvenue (PCI DSS Level 1) handles all domestic payment processing. International payments go through Wise, a regulated financial institution.
