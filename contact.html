Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! Every new organization gets a 2-day free trial with full access to all features — ATS, HRMS, AI interviews, and more. No credit card required to start.
How is my company's data secured?
We use multi-tenant row-level security on Supabase (PostgreSQL), TLS 1.3 encryption in transit, JWT-based authentication via Cloudflare Workers, and Razorpay PCI DSS Level 1 for payments. Each organization's data is completely isolated.
How long does enterprise onboarding take?
Most teams are fully onboarded within 24–48 hours. Our platform is self-serve, but enterprise clients get dedicated onboarding support including data migration, SSO setup, and team training.
How does the AI proctored interview work?
Our AI conducts 360° proctored video interviews with adaptive questions, real-time anti-cheat monitoring, and automated scoring. Supports 20+ languages and generates detailed assessment reports for HR teams.
What pricing models do you offer?
We offer Starter and Professional plans with monthly and annual billing. Enterprise clients can request custom pricing with volume discounts, dedicated support, and SLA guarantees. Visit our Pricing page for details.
Can I bring my own AI model (BYOK)?
Yes! Enterprise plans support Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) — connect your own OpenAI, Groq, or other LLM API keys for AI-powered features like resume screening and interview assessment.
Do you offer white-label or reseller partnerships?
Yes. We offer white-label licensing for HR consultancies, staffing agencies, and technology partners who want to offer our ATS & AI interview platform under their own brand. Contact our partnerships team.
