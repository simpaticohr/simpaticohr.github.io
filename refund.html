Refund Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Summary: We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. No questions asked.

1. Free Trial

All new accounts begin with a 2-day free trial with access to core features. No payment information is required during the trial period. This allows you to evaluate the platform before committing to a paid plan.

2. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to a paid plan and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. This applies to both monthly and annual subscriptions.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at hr@simpaticohrconsultancy.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Company/organization name
  • Reason for the refund (optional, helps us improve)

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

4. After the 7-Day Period

After the initial 7-day window:

  • Monthly Plans: You may cancel anytime. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for remaining days.
  • Annual Plans: Cancellations are effective at the end of the annual period. Prorated refunds may be issued on a case-by-case basis for unused months (minimum 3 months remaining).

5. Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued in cases of:

  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Account suspension due to abuse or fraudulent activity
  • Failure to cancel before auto-renewal (contact us within 48 hours of renewal for consideration)

6. International Payments (Paddle)

For international payments processed through Paddle (our Merchant of Record), refunds are handled by Paddle in accordance with their refund policies. We coordinate all refund requests on your behalf. Tax amounts collected by Paddle are also refunded proportionally.

7. Domestic Payments (Cashfree)

For domestic (INR) payments processed through Cashfree, refunds are credited to the original payment method (UPI, credit/debit card, or netbanking). Processing times may vary by bank.

8. Downgrades

If you downgrade from a higher plan to a lower plan, the change takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No refund is issued for the current period, but you retain full access to the higher plan's features until the period ends.

9. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions:

Email: hr@simpaticohrconsultancy.com
Response time: Within 24 hours on business days
